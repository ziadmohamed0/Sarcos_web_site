--- LOGO CONCEPT ---
Create a single, cohesive emblem that fuses three symbolic elements into one iconic mark:
(1) A robotic hand/arm reaching diagonally upward (representing teleoperation — human intent becoming robotic action),
(2) An ESP32 microchip die pattern embedded at the center or as the "palm" of the robotic hand (representing the MCU/RTOS core),
(3) Three concentric弧形 MQTT signal arcs radiating outward from the chip (representing wireless MQTT mesh communication).

Do NOT render these as three separate objects stacked together. They must be fused into a single, inseparable silhouette — as if the chip IS the palm of the robotic hand, and the signal arcs ARE the fingers/knuckles bending outward.

Primary accent: #00d4ff (cyan/electric blue) — for the main icon body
Secondary accent: #ffd700 (gold) — for subtle highlight on the fingertips or signal arc tips
Glow effect: A soft outer glow (#00d4ff at 20-30% opacity, blur radius ~15px) behind the icon to give it a "powered-on" cybernetic feel
Text color: Pure white (#ffffff) for "Sarco", cyan (#00d4ff) for "OS"

All elements vertically centered as a single composition block.

--- DETAILS & CONSTRAINTS ---
- The icon should look equally recognizable when scaled down to 64x64 (favicon) and at full 1024x1024
- No photorealistic gradients or 3D rendering — flat vector with crisp edges and intentional negative space
- The overall mood should be: precise, powerful, futuristic, academic yet industrial
- Think "cybersecurity company meets robotics lab" — professional, not cartoonish
- The hand should NOT look like a human hand; it should be an abstract mechanical linkage (3-4 segmented digits)
- The chip die pattern should suggest an ESP32 (two rows of visible pins on left and right edges, a small central core square)

--- RATIONALE ---
- Robotic hand = teleoperation (human-to-robot motion transfer)
- Microchip = the ESP32 + FreeRTOS/ESP-IDF firmware core
- MQTT arcs = the IoT communication layer
- Cyan = technology, precision, signal
- Gold = engineering excellence, academic achievement
- Dark background = industrial embedded-systems aesthetic, high contrast for projection screens in academic presentations

--- REJECT IF ---
- The icon looks like a generic robot face or sci-fi clip art
- The three elements (hand, chip, signal) are visually disconnected
- The color palette deviates significantly from cyan/dark/gold
- The text is hard to read when scaled down
- Any photographic or raster texture is present
